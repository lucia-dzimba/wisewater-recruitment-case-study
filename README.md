# WiseWater Media Recruitment Case Study

## Overview

This case study outlines recruitment initiatives supporting the growth of WiseWater Media. The focus was on sourcing and placing high-performing commercial and media professionals.

## Roles Recruited

• Sales Executive  
• Sales Manager  
• Operations Manager  
• Journalist  

## Recruitment Approach

The recruitment strategy included:

• Targeted talent sourcing  
• Structured candidate evaluation  
• Interview preparation frameworks  
• Role alignment with business growth objectives  

## Outcome

The recruitment initiatives supported the expansion of WiseWater Media’s commercial and editorial teams, ensuring the organization had the right talent to grow its publishing, advertising, and conferencing divisions.

## Key Recruitment Capabilities Demonstrated

• Sales recruitment expertise  
• Talent sourcing and candidate shortlisting  
• Recruitment operations design  
• Interview and evaluation frameworks
